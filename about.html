Welcome to Student Nest PG

Your second home in the city!

We understand that moving to a new place for studies or work can be exciting, but also a bit overwhelming. That's why we're here to make your transition as smooth and comfortable as possible.

Our PG isn't just about a place to stay — it's about belonging, feeling safe, and being part of a warm, welcoming community.

We offer fully furnished rooms with modern amenities like Wi-Fi, hot water, laundry facilities, and daily housekeeping. Whether you're looking for a peaceful study environment or a friendly atmosphere to make new friends, you'll find it here.

Our premises are secured with 24/7 CCTV surveillance and biometric entry, so your safety is always a top priority.
