Our Key Features

Verified Listings

Student Nest prioritizes your safety and peace of mind. That's why every property listed on our platform undergoes a thorough verification process by our dedicated field team. We personally inspect hostels and PGs to ensure they meet the highest standards of hygiene, safety, and comfort.

This includes checking security systems, basic amenities, accessibility, locality, and owner reliability. Verified listings come with a badge so you can immediately recognize and trust them. With this feature, you'll never have to worry about fake photos, misleading descriptions, or scams.

Safety Checked Hygiene Verified Authentic Photos

User Dashboard

The Student Nest app offers an intuitive and personalized User Dashboard that simplifies the entire student housing experience. From a single, user-friendly interface, students can manage everything—search for PGs/hostels, book a room, track their payment history, view upcoming appointments, save favorites, and communicate directly with property owners.

The dashboard is specially designed with students in mind, featuring a clean layout, colorful icons, and mobile responsiveness for easy use on the go. It also sends important reminders about check-in dates, upcoming rent payments, and feedback requests, ensuring you stay organized and informed.

Easy Booking Payment Tracking Mobile Friendly

Trusted Ratings

Making a decision about where to stay is easier when you hear from people who've actually been there. That's why Student Nest features Trusted Ratings from verified students who have stayed at the listed PGs or hostels. Every review is carefully moderated to ensure authenticity—only users who've booked through our platform can rate and share their experience.

You get real feedback about cleanliness, food quality, landlord behavior, neighborhood safety, and more. Ratings are displayed as detailed scores and honest written comments, offering a 360-degree view of each property. It helps future users understand the pros and cons before making a choice. These reviews aren't just for show—they influence how listings appear in search results and help hold property owners accountable for quality.

Authentic Reviews Detailed Feedback Verified Students

Virtual Tours

Student Nest brings your future room to your screen through immersive virtual tours. This feature allows students to explore properties in detail from the comfort of their current location. Whether it's walking through a room, checking the common areas, or inspecting the neighborhood, virtual tours give a transparent and interactive experience.

The 360-degree views help in understanding the exact layout and condition of the property before booking. It saves time, builds trust, and removes the anxiety of the unknown. Especially for students moving from far-off locations, this is a game-changer. With Virtual Tours, what you see is truly what you get.

360° Views Interactive Time Saver
